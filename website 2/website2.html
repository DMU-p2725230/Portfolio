About Me

I am an 18 year old Media Production student currently studying at DeMontfort Uniersity. My experience in media starting during secondary school, where I chose to study it for GCSE's, then leading on to further study the subject at Peterborough College, where I earned the grades to be accepted into DeMontfort University.

Over the years that I have studied Media I have done projects such as film television advertisements and music videos, recorded a podcast with a member of an environmental group local to Peterborough called PECT, recorded a live radio show as well as two seperate live television shows,one at college and one at University.

Outside of education, I gained media industry experience when completing a week of work experience in London when I worked as a runner, as well as camera operating on a shoot for Epic Games, in March of 2022. I have also been the photographer at a family wedding in July of 2019.
